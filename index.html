About NYBN

A different kind of room.

NYBN was created for the conversations that rarely happen at large industry events. We bring together a curated group of New York GCs, subcontractors, and category-leading partners in smaller settings built around trust, usefulness, and long-term relationships.

01

Curated by category

Partner seats are filled by invitation and intentionally limited by discipline. The result is a room of providers members can actually trust to be useful.

02

Small by design

Gatherings are kept intentionally intimate. Real conversation, considered introductions, and the kind of context you only get face to face.

03

Built around trust

Members and partners show up to be helpful, not to sell. Discretion is the default, and relationships are designed to compound well past the event.
